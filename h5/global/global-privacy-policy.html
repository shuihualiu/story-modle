Privacy Policy

Global edition · Package: com.haloutongxue.reminder
Last updated: June 21, 2025

Welcome to Plan Together! We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use Plan Together outside mainland China. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account information

When you register or sign in, we may collect your email address, nickname, avatar, and authorized third-party account information from Google Sign-In or Sign in with Apple (where available). Guest identifiers may be used before you link an account.

1.2 Feature data

Plans, tasks, schedules, anniversaries, habits, focus sessions, reminders, statistics, preferences, and other content you create or use in the app.

1.3 Device and log data

Device model, OS version, app version, network status, push token, and diagnostic logs used to secure the service and improve stability.

1.4 Shipping information

Recipient name, phone number, and shipping address, only when you submit them for blind-box prizes or delivery.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide core app services, including synchronization, reminders, and analytics.
  • Improve the product and user experience through analysis of non-identifiable usage data.
  • Security and compliance: to prevent abuse, detect anomalies, and protect accounts and data.
  • Notifications: with your authorization, we may send service-related notifications through Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM).

3. Storage and Protection

We store personal information with appropriate security measures such as encryption and access controls. Data is primarily processed through our international cloud infrastructure (Supabase). We retain information only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy and as required by applicable law.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted providers to operate the app under lawful and necessary purposes. See Section 9 below for third-party details.

5. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights such as access, correction, deletion, data portability, and withdrawing consent where applicable. You may close your account through in-app options or by contacting us.

6. Children

Plan Together is not directed to children under the age required in your jurisdiction to consent to online services without parental approval.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated in the app. Continued use after an update constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us via in-app Feedback in Plan Together.

9. Personal Information Collection List

This list describes the types of personal information that Plan Together may collect when providing planning, reminders, statistics, and related features outside mainland China. We only collect information necessary for our services.

Account & identity

  • Email address: sign-in, verification, and account recovery.
  • Google / Apple account identifiers and authorized profile data (e.g. nickname, avatar, email): third-party sign-in and account linking.
  • Anonymous user identifier: guest identity and data association before you link email or a third-party account.
  • Server user identifier: authentication and cloud sync.

Feature data

  • Plans, tasks, schedules, anniversaries, habits, focus sessions, and other content you create.
  • Reminder rules, notification preferences, language and UI settings.
  • Membership status and entitlement records.
  • Recipient name, phone number, and shipping address: only when you submit them for blind-box prizes or delivery.

Device & logs

  • Device model, OS version, app version, network status, and push token.
  • Crash and diagnostic logs to improve stability.
  • Local notification, alarm, and home-screen widget settings.

Images & media

  • Avatars, plan images, and memorial-board photos you upload or pick from the gallery with your permission.

Information we do not actively collect

We do not actively collect contacts, SMS, call logs, precise location trails, or biometrics unrelated to core features unless we obtain your explicit consent for a new feature.

How information is collected

  • Information you enter or upload.
  • Data obtained after you grant permissions (e.g. photo library, notifications).
  • Technical logs required for secure and stable operation.
